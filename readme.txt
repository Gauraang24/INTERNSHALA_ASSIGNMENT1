This project is a personal blog by Gaurang More, a frontend developer who enjoys reading books. The blog offers concise summaries of three popular books: "Atomic Habits," "7 Habits of Highly Effective People," and "Rich Dad Poor Dad."

Content:
Each book summary provides key insights and takeaways. The blog also includes sections introducing Gaurang and offering contact information for readers to reach out.

Media Usage:
To maintain quality and comfort, Gaurang sourced images and videos from the internet instead of creating original content.
